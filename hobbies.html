My Hobbies

The Gym

Going to the gym relaxes my mind, helps me build discipline, and keeps me healthy. I enjoy setting personal fitness goals and pushing myself to improve strength, endurance, and overall wellness. It’s a great way to clear my head and stay focused both physically and mentally.

Basketball

Playing basketball gives me excellent cardio and allows me to have fun while staying active. I am very competitive when I play and it helps bring that part of me out. Basketball also helps me build my teamworking skills.

Hanging Out with Friends

Spending time with friends makes me happy and helps relieve stress. Whether it’s going out to eat, playing games, or watching sports games, these moments help me a lot mentally, strengthen relationships, and create lasting memories.
